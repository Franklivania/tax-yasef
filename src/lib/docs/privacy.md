## 1. Privacy Policy

### 1.1 Information We Collect

Tax Yasef is built with privacy-first principles. We may collect the following types of information:

**a. Information You Provide Voluntarily**

- Income figures and tax-related inputs entered into the calculator
- Questions submitted when chatting with Taxman (AI Assistant)
- Email addresses provided for newsletters, reports, or downloads
- Feature requests or feedback submitted through the platform

**b. Automatically Collected Information**

- Browser type and device information
- Anonymous usage data (pages visited, time spent)
- Temporary calculation history stored locally in your browser (MVP phase)

> **Important:** Tax calculations are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to generate, email, or download a report.

---

### 1.2 How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Perform accurate tax calculations
- Generate tax reports requested by users
- Improve platform accuracy, usability, and features
- Provide educational tax content and updates
- Respond to support or feature requests

**We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.**

---

### 1.3 AI & Chat Feature (Taxman)

Taxman is an AI-powered assistant trained on:

- Nigerian tax laws (including the 2025 Nigerian Tax Act)
- General tax guidance
- User-provided inputs during conversations

**Key Notes:**

- Conversations may be reviewed anonymously to improve accuracy and safety.
- Taxman does _not_ replace professional tax advisors or legal practitioners.
- Responses are informational, not legally binding advice.

---

### 1.4 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share limited data _only when necessary_ with:

- Email delivery services (for reports and newsletters)
- Analytics providers (in anonymized form)
- Government platforms (only when you are redirected to file taxes externally)

We do **not** share user data with tax authorities or government agencies automatically.

---

### 1.5 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data, including:

- Secure servers and encrypted connections
- Restricted internal access to sensitive information
- Regular platform monitoring

However, no system is 100% secure. Users are encouraged to avoid submitting highly sensitive personal identifiers (e.g., BVN, NIN) unless explicitly required by an official government platform.

---

### 1.6 Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Request deletion of your data
- Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time
- Request clarification on how your data is used

Requests can be sent via the contact details provided on the platform.

## 2. Compliance Notice

- Tax Yasef operates as an informational and preparatory tool, _not_ a government tax authority.
- We align our calculations and explanations with current Nigerian tax laws, but tax laws may change.
- Tax Yasef links users to official government portals for final tax filing.
- We are not affiliated with the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) or any state tax authority.

**Users remain fully responsible for:**

- Verifying submitted tax information
- Filing taxes through official government channels
- Ensuring compliance with local, state, and federal tax obligations

### 3.1 No Professional Advice

Tax Yasef does **not** provide:

- Legal advice

_All information, calculations, AI responses, and reports are provided for educational and informational purposes only._

You should consult a qualified tax professional, accountant, or lawyer before making financial or tax-related decisions.
