### Self-Assessment

#### What is Self-Assessment?

Self-assessment is a tax system where individuals calculate and declare their own tax liability for the year, instead of relying solely on employer deductions.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Freelancers**
- **Independent contractors**
- **Salary earners with side income**
- **Individuals earning rent or investment income**
- **People with foreign (overseas) income**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- Personal Income Tax filings with your State Internal Revenue Service (SIRS)
- Typically filed annually

---

#### Why Is Self-Assessment Important?

- **PAYE** (Pay-As-You-Earn) only captures salary income, not other income types
- Enables reporting of multiple income sources in one return
- Ensures people pay tax on earnings that aren't taxed at source
