#### What is it?

Withholding Tax (WHT) using the credit method means that tax is withheld (deducted) at source from payments you receive for services. However, this amount is treated as a prepayment and credited against your final tax liability when you file your annual tax return.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Freelancers**
- **Consultants**
- **Contractors**
- **Service providers**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- Payments received for professional or contract services
- Credited during annual self-assessment and tax reconciliation

---

#### Why Does It Exist?

- Ensures the government collects tax early
- Reduces tax evasion
- Prevents delays in full tax payment by spreading payments throughout the year
