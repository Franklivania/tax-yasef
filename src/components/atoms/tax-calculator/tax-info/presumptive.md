### Presumptive Tax

#### What is it?

Presumptive tax is a simplified method of tax assessment used when proper records are unavailable and true income cannot be accurately determined.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Informal traders**
- **Small businesses with poor or no records**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- **Informal sector** operations
- Situations where tax authorities estimate income due to lack of records

---

#### Why Does It Exist?

- Many small businesses lack bookkeeping practices
- Helps prevent total tax loss (leakage)
- Encourages informal businesses to gradually become formal and maintain better records
