### Business / Trade Income (Individual)

#### What is it?

Business/trade income tax applies to profits earned from business activities or professional practices, not from salaried employment.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Sole proprietors**
- **Small business owners**
- **Professionals running their own practices**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- Included in **annual Personal Income Tax** filings
- Tax is computed on profits (income minus allowable expenses) before applying tax bands
