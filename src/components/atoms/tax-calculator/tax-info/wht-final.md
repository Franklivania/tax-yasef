### Withholding Tax – Final Tax (WHT Final)

#### What is it?

Withholding Tax (WHT) as a final tax means the tax is deducted at source just once—after that, no further tax reporting or payments are required for that particular income. The tax deduction fully settles your tax liability on the income it covers.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Dividend recipients**
- **Certain non-resident income earners**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- Investment income (such as dividends or interest)
- Cross-border payments

---

#### Why Does It Exist?

- Simplifies the tax process for certain income types
- Eliminates the need for repeated tax filings on that income
- Efficient for passive or one-off income streams
