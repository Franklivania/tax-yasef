### VAT – Collection / Self-Accounting

#### What is it?

Value Added Tax (VAT) is a consumption tax assessed on goods and services, rather than on income. It is typically collected at each stage of the supply chain—from production, to distribution, to the final sale.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Businesses**
- **Service providers**
- **Importers**
- **Non-resident suppliers to Nigeria**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- Sale of taxable goods and services
- Monthly VAT filings and remittances

---

#### Why Does It Exist?

- Taxes spending/consumption, not earnings
- Broadens the government's tax base
- Easier for authorities to collect at the point of transaction
