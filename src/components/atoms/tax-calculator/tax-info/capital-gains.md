### Capital Gains Tax

#### What is it?

Capital Gains Tax applies to the profit made from selling assetsâ€”such as property, shares, or digital assets. It is different from taxes on regular income or salary.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Property sellers**
- **Shareholders**
- **Digital asset traders** (e.g. cryptocurrency, NFTs)

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- When you dispose of (sell or transfer) assets
- Tax is calculated per qualifying transaction

---

#### Why Does It Exist?

- Prevents untaxed accumulation of wealth through the sale of assets
- Ensures tax is captured on gains outside of regular income
