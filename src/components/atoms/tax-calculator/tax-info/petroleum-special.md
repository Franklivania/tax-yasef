### Petroleum / Special Sector Taxes

#### What is it?

Certain industries—such as petroleum, mining, insurance, and other highly regulated sectors—are subject to special tax laws and calculation methods. These rules differ significantly from standard income tax provisions.

---

#### Who Does It Affect?

- **Oil & gas operators**
- **Mining companies**
- **Insurance firms**
- **Gaming, lotteries, and other regulated industries**

---

#### Where Does It Apply?

- Sector-specific business operations
- Federal (and sometimes state) tax filings for regulated activities

---

#### Why Does It Exist?

- To address the unique value and risk profiles of key industries
- To account for specialized operating costs
- To safeguard and maximize national revenue from strategic sectors
